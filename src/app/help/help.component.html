Game Help & Guide

Little Linguist App is an educational tool which helps you with your first steps in learning English.

In this app, there are 2 modes:

  • Admin Mode: Add, edit, and delete word categories.
  • Game Mode: Practice your English.

The app has two games:

  • Word Sorting Game: For each word, the player has to choose whether it belongs to the category or not.
  • Mixed Letters Game: For each word from the category, the meaning is in Hebrew and the English word letters are shown in a messed order. The user has to rearrange the letters in the correct order.
